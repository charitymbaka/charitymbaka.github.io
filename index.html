Who is Charity?

Charity Mbaka is a GIS and front-end developer. She is enthusiastic about technology and data, and how we can leverage the former to gain meaningful insights from the latter.

She has a B.Sc Degree in Geospatial Information Science. She has worked/interned for a game development startup, a GIS research firm, GIS and Land Survey firm, and is currently a GIS developer at a cyber security RnD hub.

In her free time she contributes to technology blogs: Kenyan-based TechMoran and Nigeria-based Innovation Village. She also volunteers to digitize maps for cause via HotOSM at mapathons.

Charity is constantly working to improve her skills. She is a fast learner and a dilligent worker, whose number one priority is to provide the best quality work using open source tools.
